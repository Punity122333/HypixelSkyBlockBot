üéØ CREATING AN AUCTION:

1. Open /auction menu
2. Click "Create Auction"
3. Select item from inventory
4. Set starting bid (minimum 1 coin)
5. Choose duration (1h, 6h, 12h, 24h, 48h)
6. Optional: Set BIN price for instant sale
7. Confirm and list

AUCTION FEES:
- 1% listing fee (min 1 coin)
- 2% sale fee on completion
- Fees help maintain economy
- BIN has no extra fees
