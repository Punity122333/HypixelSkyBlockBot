The Co-op (Cooperative) System allows players to team up permanently and share resources, island, and progress.

COMMANDS:
/coop - Manage your co-op with all features

WHAT IS A CO-OP?
A co-op is a permanent alliance where members share:
- Shared bank account
- Shared minions
- Shared island
- Collaborative progression

KEY DIFFERENCES FROM PARTIES:
Party - Temporary group for dungeons only
Co-op - Permanent alliance with shared resources

CREATING A CO-OP:
1. Use /coop command
2. Click "Create Co-op" button
3. Enter a name for your co-op
4. You become the owner with full permissions
5. You earn the "Cooperative" badge ü§ù

IMPORTANT: You can only be in ONE co-op at a time!

  - Manage members

  - Use bank

  - Use bank

  - Invite members

Member (üë§) - Regular co-op members:
  - Use bank
  - Manage minions

Guest - Limited access, no permissions

INVITING MEMBERS:
1. Open /coop menu
2. Click "Invite" button (Owner/Admin only)
3. Enter the User ID of the player
4. They receive an invitation
5. Only owners and admins can invite

PERMISSIONS SYSTEM:
Each role has specific permissions that control what they can do:

manage_members - Add/remove co-op members
manage_permissions - Change member roles
use_bank - Deposit/withdraw from shared bank
manage_minions - Place and collect from minions
manage_island - Build and modify the island
invite_members - Send invitations
kick_members - Remove members from co-op

SHARED BANK:
The co-op has a shared bank account separate from personal coins:

Depositing:
1. Navigate to Bank view
2. Click "Deposit"
3. Enter amount
4. Coins transferred from personal to shared bank

Withdrawing:
1. Navigate to Bank view
2. Click "Withdraw"
3. Enter amount
4. Coins transferred from shared to personal

Bank Features:
- Default capacity: 10,000 coins
- All members with permission can use it
- View current balance and capacity
- Usage percentage displayed
- Protected by permission system

SHARED MINIONS:
Co-op members share minions placed on the island:

- All minions are visible to co-op members
- Members with permission can place minions
- Members with permission can collect from minions
- Shared minion slots across the co-op
- Coordinate with team for optimal minion placement

LEAVING A CO-OP:
1. Open /coop menu
2. Click "Leave" button
3. Confirm you want to leave
4. You lose access to shared resources
5. If owner leaves, co-op may be disbanded

KICKING MEMBERS:
(Owner/Admin only with kick_members permission)
1. Must have proper permissions
2. Use invite/manage system
3. Kicked members lose access immediately
4. Kicked members keep personal progress

CHANGING ROLES:
(Owner only with manage_permissions)
