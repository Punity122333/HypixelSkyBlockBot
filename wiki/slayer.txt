üìä SLAYER BASICS:

WHAT ARE SLAYERS:
- Special boss encounters
- Require slayer quest activation
- Reward slayer XP and unique drops
- Four slayer types available
- Each has multiple tiers (I-V)

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Activate a slayer quest (costs coins)
2. Kill regular mobs to spawn the boss
3. Defeat the slayer boss
4. Earn slayer XP and rewards
5. Unlock higher tiers and perks
