🏰 Dungeons Guide

Dungeons are challenging procedurally-generated instances where you fight mobs, find secrets, and earn valuable loot!

📍 THE CATACOMBS:
Dungeon floors range from Entrance to Floor 7, plus Master Mode floors.

🎮 HOW TO ENTER:
Use /dungeon <floor> to start a dungeon run

🔍 SECRETS:
Search for secrets throughout the dungeon:
- Chests
- Levers
- Wither Essence
- Bats
- Item Frames
- And more!

Note: Secrets found in dungeons are for scoring and rewards, but don't count as permanent fairy soul collections.

🚪 NAVIGATION:
- Open Door: Move through rooms
- Search for Secrets: Find hidden items
- Exit Dungeon: Complete your run

📊 SCORING:
Your dungeon score is based on:
- Rooms cleared
- Secrets found (each secret worth 5 points)
- Health remaining
- Deaths (25 point penalty each)
- Puzzles completed
- Doors unlocked
- Crypts opened

🎁 REWARDS:
- Better scores = Better loot
- Magic Find affects drops
- Coins based on performance
- Special dungeon items
- Experience for Catacombs level

💡 TIPS:
- Search every room for secrets
- Avoid taking damage
- Don't die (heavy score penalty)
- Unlock all doors you find
- Team up with a party for harder floors

📈 PROGRESSION:
Start with Entrance and work your way up as you:
- Improve your gear
- Level your Catacombs level
- Learn the mechanics
- Build your combat skills

Use /dungeon_stats to track your progress!
