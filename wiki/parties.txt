The Party System allows players to team up with friends for dungeons and other group activities.

COMMANDS:
/party - Manage your party with an interactive menu
/party_finder - Browse and join dungeon parties

CREATING A PARTY:
1. Use /party command
2. Click "Create Party" button
3. Your party is created with you as the leader
4. You can invite up to 4 other players (5 total members)

PARTY ROLES:
Leader (ðŸ‘‘) - The party creator who can:
  - Invite players to the party
  - Kick members from the party
  - Start dungeon runs
  - Disband the party

Members - Can:
  - Leave the party at any time
  - Participate in dungeon runs
  - Choose their dungeon class

INVITING PLAYERS:
1. Open your party menu with /party
2. Click "Invite" button
3. Enter the User ID of the player you want to invite
4. They will receive an invite that expires in 5 minutes
5. They can accept or decline the invite

LEAVING A PARTY:
1. Open /party menu
2. Click "Leave Party"
3. If you're the leader, leadership transfers to the next member
4. If you're the last member, the party is disbanded
5. Cannot leave during an active dungeon run

PARTY FINDER:
The Party Finder helps you find or create dungeon parties:

BROWSING PARTIES:
- View all open parties looking for members
- See party details: leader, members, floor, requirements
- Recommended class shown for each party
- Use Previous/Next to navigate pages

JOINING A PARTY:
1. Open /party_finder
2. Click "Join Party"
3. Enter the Party ID
4. Choose your dungeon class:
   - Healer - Support role
   - Mage - Magic damage
   - Berserker - High physical damage
   - Archer - Ranged damage
   - Tank - High defense

CREATING DUNGEON PARTIES:
1. Open /party_finder
2. Click "Create Party"
3. Choose floor (1-7)
4. Set minimum Catacombs level (optional)
5. Add description (optional)

DUNGEON CLASSES:
Each class provides different bonuses in dungeons:

Berserker - 1.5x damage multiplier
Mage - 1.3x damage multiplier
Archer - 1.2x damage multiplier
Healer - Support abilities
Tank - High survivability

STARTING A DUNGEON:
1. Form a party (1-5 members)
2. Leader uses /party_finder or /dungeon command
3. Click "Start Dungeon"
4. All members must meet level requirements
5. Dungeon begins with randomly generated rooms

DUNGEON MECHANICS:
- Multiple room types: Combat, Puzzle, Trap, Miniboss, Boss
- Clear rooms to progress through the dungeon
- Party damage is combined for combat rooms
- Score increases as you clear rooms
- Cannot leave party during active run

ROOM TYPES:
Mob Room - Fight multiple enemies
Puzzle Room - Solve puzzles to progress
Trap Room - Survive environmental hazards
Miniboss Room - Fight a stronger enemy
Boss Room - Final challenge (always last room)

DUNGEON REWARDS:
- Base reward: 1000 coins Ã— floor number

- Rewards split between party members
- Higher floors = better rewards
- Completing all rooms gives bonus

PARTY TIPS:
- Balance your party with different classes
- Healer recommended for harder floors
- Tank helps party survive longer
- Communication is key for dungeons
- Higher gear score = easier clears

PARTY STATES:
Open - Accepting new members
In Progress - Currently in a dungeon
Full - Maximum members reached (5/5)

RESTRICTIONS:
- Cannot invite bots
- Cannot kick yourself
- Cannot change party during dungeon
- Cannot kick members during dungeon
- Invites expire after 5 minutes
- Must be party leader to start dungeon
